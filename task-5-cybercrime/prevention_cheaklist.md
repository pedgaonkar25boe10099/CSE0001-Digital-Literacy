Stay Safe Online — Cybercrime Prevention Checklist
For College Students in India

General Online Safety

Always verify the sender's email address before clicking any link
Never click on links in unsolicited emails or SMS messages
Check that website URLs begin with "https://" before entering any data
Use strong, unique passwords for each online account
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
Log out of accounts when using shared or public computers

Financial & UPI Safety

 Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank password with anyone — not even someone claiming to be from your bank
 Be cautious of "collect requests" on UPI apps — you never need to enter your PIN to receive money
 Verify the receiver's UPI ID carefully before every transaction
 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for any banking or payment activity

Reporting

 National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
 Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
 Report any suspicious activity immediately — early reporting increases the chance of recovering lost funds.
